Why chose Mobile Escapes?

Most people have already participated in this extremely popular team building activity. However, planning that event isn’t always an easy task. With us being mobile, you can simply stay an extra hour at work or include us in your private party schedule for one hour of excitement.

What type of room do you need?

Generally, any room that can accommodate 6-10 people can be used. Here’s a list of the most common rooms: Classroom, boardroom, office, living room, bedroom, basement, staff room, etc.

How much time does it take to set up/take down the room?

Approximately 30 minutes to set up and 15 minutes to take down.

Do you travel?

Yes. We only charge travelling expenses when you are more than 30 minutes away from Orléans, Ontario.

Do we need to empty the room?

Taking out valuable objects from your room is encouraged. However, we are always present in the room during the escape to make sure the participants only used our props to complete their challenge.

Can we offer this experience as a gift?

Yes! This is the perfect gift! We will make a custom gift certificate for you to give.

What is the minimum age group?

We have escapes built for 10 years old and up.
