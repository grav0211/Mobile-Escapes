The Escape Inn

Mobile Escapes was officially launched on October 30th 2016 with a Halloween themed event where more than 100 people participated. It was hosted at Station 4 Saisons, in Hammond, Ontario, and a total of 12 teams battled to escape with the fastest time. A big thanks to Station 4 Saisons for the amazing food, drinks and service.

Escape Details

  • Participants: 12 teams of 6-10 people
  • Room used: 2 hotel rooms
